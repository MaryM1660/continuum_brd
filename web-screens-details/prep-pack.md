# Prep Pack Screen

**Route:** `/prep-pack/:id`

## Overview

The Prep Pack is a curated set of interview preparation materials assembled for a specific job interview. It pulls from the Career Profile the most relevant STARL stories and XYZ achievements for the specific role, and adds interview-specific context.

---

## Header

- Company + role + interview date/type
- E.g., "Acme Inc. · Product Manager · Feb 12, 10am · Technical interview"
- Interview type badge: Phone Screen | Technical | Behavioral | Case Study | Final Round

---
